CPA (Cost per Action)

CPA model - payment for each action taken by a user. Terms:

  • $1 for each registration brought by you.
  • $15 for a deposit of $75 or more.
  • For every 50 participants brought in, there must be at least one deposit of $75 or more.
  • Payment is made once all conditions are met, without delays.
  • Partners with high activity levels may qualify for additional bonuses and awards.

Deposit levels and corresponding payments:

  • Deposit from $75: $15 per deposit.
  • Deposit from $200: $40 per deposit + additional bonus for bringing 30 new users.
  • Deposit from $500: $100 per deposit + bonus for bringing 50 new users.
  • Deposit from $1000: $250 per deposit + bonus for bringing 100 new users.

For each deposit level, you need to bring a certain number of new users. Example:

  • For a deposit of $200, you need to bring 30 new users.
  • For a deposit of $500, you need to bring 50 new users.
  • For a deposit of $1000, you need to bring 100 new users.

This model is perfect for those who actively attract users and can ensure a steady flow of new registrations and deposits. As the deposit increases, so do the bonuses!

Rev Share (Revenue Share)

The Rev Share model is based on earning a percentage of the revenue generated by users you brought to the platform. A user is defined as someone who has made a deposit of $75 or more. The more active users you have, the higher your income. Terms:

  • 5% of all deposits from the users you brought.
  • 6.89% of all deposits from the users you brought and who remain active for more than 30 days.
  • Additional bonuses for bringing a certain number of new users:
    • 100 new users: $83 bonus.
    • 500 new users: $250 bonus.
    • 1000 new users: $500 bonus.

This model allows you to earn from the long-term activity of the users you attracted, as well as receive bonuses for high performance in attracting and retaining users. Partners who can attract a significant number of users will receive substantial bonuses.

Agent

The Agent model is designed for those who invite new partners and earn a percentage of their partners' income. Agents receive commissions based on the total earnings of the partners they bring in.

  • 10% of the earnings from partners' closed deals.
  • 15% if you have invited more than 50 active partners.
  • 20% if you have invited more than 100 active partners.
  • Additional bonuses for attracting high-earning partners and working with top-tier affiliates.

Agents also get access to exclusive offers and additional rewards for outstanding results in attracting and supporting partners. This model is ideal for those who want to build a long-term, scalable income by developing their own network of successful partners.
